About us

We are a young and dynamic company founded with the purpose of providing the best postural and mobility solutions regarding clients needs.

Children's posture is often ignored when transported in vehicles because carers don't have access to the right equipment. On the other hand, a wheelchair adapted vehicle is not always an option considered or affordable by parents.
